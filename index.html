FAQ's

Still have questions?

Feel to reach out! We are always excited to hear from you.

What makes you different from other WordPress agencies in India?

Our key difference lies in our approach: we are your strategic partners, not just developers. We combine global best practices with a deep understanding of the Indian market. Our focus is on achieving your business goals be it lead generation, e-commerce sales, or brand authority through a transparent and collaborative process.

Do you offer fixed-price packages or custom quotes?

While we have standard pricing for some services like maintenance, most of our design and development projects are unique. Therefore, we provide a detailed custom quote in INR after a free consultation to understand your specific requirements. This ensures you only pay for what you need.

What kind of support do you offer after the website is launched?

We provide 30 days of complimentary support post-launch to address any bugs. Beyond that, we offer flexible Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMCs) that cover regular backups, security monitoring, and software updates to keep your digital asset in perfect health.

Are the websites you build secure?

Security is a top priority. We implement a multi-layered security strategy on every website we build, including secure coding practices, firewalls, malware scanning, and regular updates, to protect your site from common threats.

How long does a typical WordPress project take?

A standard website build takes between 7-14 days. A complex WooCommerce or custom development project might take longer. We will always provide a clear and detailed project timeline before we begin any work.

My current WordPress site is very slow. Can you help fix it?

Absolutely. We specialize in WordPress performance optimization. We conduct a comprehensive audit to identify bottlenecks and then implement advanced solutions like code optimization, image compression, database cleanup, and server-level caching to dramatically improve your website's loading speed and Core Web Vitals.
